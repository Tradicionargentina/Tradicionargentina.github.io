ARGENTINE TRADITIONAL DAY

Enjoy a good wine and the best Argentine meat cooked on the grill, in a country setting, quiet and cozy.

A great day to leave the city behind

It is said that a good barbecue should be eaten outdoors accompanied by good wine. It should be enjoyed little by little: it is not worth serving a plate full of cuts that get cold while we eat. It is preferable to go to the grill several times and thus bring to the table small delights that we will enjoy hot and crispy.

Our traditional gastronomic day begins with a trip to the outskirts of the city, less than an hour away from the Obelisco, to the town of Cañuelas where we will be waiting for you. When you arrive at our establishment you will descend from the vehicle to stretch your legs, take a big breath of fresh air and while you release it slowly, you can walk relaxed to go around the place by yourself. Then you will approach the table specially prepared to taste homemade empanadas and a traditional “picada” (a plate of regional cold meats and cheeses served as an appetizer), accompanied by a drink...a good wine?...why not?.

Later, a short but relaxed walk to the grill where you will meet our expert "parrillero" who will have been preparing from very early a delicacy with the best cuts of meat cooked on the grill. It’s the perfect time to choose a succulent portion ... and there you go ... to enjoy your barbecue. More wine? ... of course! ... A good barbecue, accompanied by a good wine, enhances its flavor.

What’s next? Well, simply relax and enjoy the place while children have fun in the playground or visiting the mini farm .. go ahead! You are free to relax as you like.
But the day does not end without first having an afternoon snack to taste the elixir of the Argentine sweets, the traditional dulce de leche. Cañuelas has the honor of being the place of origin of dulce de leche (or at least that's its history tells us). It is the place where the Provincial Fiesta del Dulce de Leche is celebrated, attracting locals and tourists, eager to try the best dulce de leche in the world.
